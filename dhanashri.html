Dhanashri

We have become so engrossed in the traditional education system that we have forgotten to impart financial and vocational knowledge to our children. A 22-year-old post graduate today does not even know how to pay a check. So we are launching Dhanashri through which children can understand things related to finance from an early age and further they can become good professionals.

Also We Do Novel Things under Dhanshri like

  • To make young people self-reliant by providing loans at reasonable rates who want to start a new business. (Interest rate is Determined under RBI rule)
  • Building houses in vacant space and selling them at reasonable prices.
  • Purchase and sale of vacant plots.
  • 30% of the proceeds will be used for organizing community wedding ceremonies for the daughters of farmers below the poverty line.
