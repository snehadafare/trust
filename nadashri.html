Nadashri

Music is one of the oldest and most popular arts in India. In today's world there are many platforms for presenting it. All that is required is to master this art in a pure way. We help children from a very young age to have a career in this by imparting commercial knowledge.

Also, Swargandha Nakshatra Swarancha is A Show Presented By.
This is a wonderful program of singing Marathi and Hindi songs. This is a program performed by talented artists and professional singers, narrators and musicians from Kalashri, Vidyashri. Through this group, performances of Mars occasions and independent concerts are done. Independent programs like Gajlar Rang, Bhav Spandan, Gatha Shoryachi, Diwali Pahat, Sant Panchayat are also given.

About 30% of the proceeds go to camps for poor budding citizens with health problems and free health care services.
