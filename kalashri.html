Kalashri

Art is standing with one hand extended into the universe and one hand extended into the world, and letting ourselves be a conduit for passing energy. That's what Albert Einstein said.

Art is a gift from God to a person which never ends and if the power of this art is used properly, it can become the greatest way to become the best in the world. That is why we teach children to commercialize art through Kalashri.
