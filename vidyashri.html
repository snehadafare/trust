Vidyashri

In this category we have included Life Education along with traditional education which also includes things from business and public life. We have fully considered how the student who is smart in this will make overall progress through this.

Vidyashree Public School is not only an educational institute but also a unique education system with quality education and art education for children in rural areas. including Indian classical music, and Warkari sectarian education along with personality development Behavioral education Including all "Shri Seva Prakalp" is running at a modest fee of Rs. 3,000 only.

Semi english courses, English medium schools, cbse courses, international courses, schools with large franchises, their expensive fees and donations In such a situation, parents in rural areas where there is drought, indebtedness, farmers commit suicide, parents in such areas cannot admit their children to such institutions with such high fees. and the educational gap that arises from this is devastating to the human race. With this in mind, the Shri Manav Vikas Charitable Trust has set out to create the perfect personality and the ideal citizen of India through the "Shri Seva Prakalp".

Along with this educational awareness, we are also running the Save Soil and Tree campaign as a social responsibility. There is no doubt that our commitment to plant 10 million trees will be fulfilled.
